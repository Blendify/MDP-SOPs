Maryland Productions SOP Handbook
*********************************

Welcome to the Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) Handbook for **Maryland Productions** and
**Event Revolution (REV)**. This handbook provides clear, consistent, and standardized procedures
for all operational, technical, and administrative workflows across both organizations.

The goal of this documentation is to ensure:

- Reliable and repeatable processes.
- Clear communication between departments.
- Safe and efficient warehouse, logistics, and on-site operations.
- Accurate asset tracking for MP and RV inventory.
- Consistent show quality across all client engagements.
- Faster onboarding for new staff and freelancers.
- A single source of truth for all procedures.

This handbook is intended for:
- Full-time staff  
- Warehouse and logistics teams  
- Technicians and freelancers  
- Project managers  
- Sales and administrative staff  

Each section below contains detailed SOPs, templates, checklists, and references for the workflow
it covers.


Using This Handbook
===================

- Expand sections in the sidebar to navigate into detailed SOPs.
- Use search to quickly locate procedures, terms, or tasks.
- Refer to linked templates and checklists as part of daily operations.
- All SOPs follow a consistent structure: Purpose, Scope, Responsibilities, Procedure, and QC.

Document Conventions
====================

- **MP** and **RV** indicate Maryland Productions and Event Revolution asset ownership.
- **Flex** refers to the asset management system used by REV.
- **Current RMS** is the rental management system used by MP.
